OSCAR

Are you finally satisfied? — he held her by the waist like a glass with the last sip of night still swirling inside.
She looked at him, eyes narrowed, as if deciding whether to tell the truth or leave it unsaid, like an intentional artistic choice.
Millions read me. Millions fall in love with my words. But only you... — she traced his lips with her finger, — only you know how my voice sounds without editing.
He smirked, and his smile became that final line — the one that turns a novel into a cult classic.
P.S. Her triumph wasn’t the fee or the fame. Her triumph was that in the morning, he was still there.
